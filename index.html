Welcome to the garden!

A six-piece growing something loud in the London jazz scene, with afrobeat grooves, jungle breaks and grungy rock folded into joyful, colour-soaked live shows.

The Happy Garden EP

We're so proud to have finally released our first EP "Happy garden" earlier this year!
