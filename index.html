Bio

Explosive, high-energy, and impossible to pin down.

Happy Garden are a six-piece pushing the boundaries of contemporary jazz in the London scene. Blending organic grooves, intricate motifs and rich harmonies, the band's dynamic live shows move fluidly between introspective melodies and dancy afrobeat, grungy rock and energetic jungle, captivating audiences with joyful, colourful soundscapes.

Since forming, they've taken their sound from packed basement shows at The Lower Third in Soho to the main stage of the EFG London Jazz Festival at the Barbican and the legendary stage at Ronnie Scott's Jazz Club, building a following defined by word of mouth and unmissable live sets. This is an act that rewards seeing live.
