Bio

Explosive, high-energy, and impossible to pin down.

Happy Garden are a six-piece pushing the boundaries of jazz in the London scene. Blending organic grooves, intricate motifs and rich harmonies, the band's dynamic live shows move fluidly between introspective melodies and dancy afrobeat, grungy rock and energetic jungle, captivating audiences with joyful, colourful soundscapes.

Since forming, they've taken their sound from packed basement shows at The Lower Third in Soho, to two sold out shows at Ronnie Scott's Jazz Club; to festival stages such as EFG London Jazz Festival and Shambala. Building a following from the ground up through unmissable live sets, word of mouth, and a strong social media presence, Happy garden are taking the UK music scene by storm. This is an act that cannot be missed.
